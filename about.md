## About EduGrid

EduGrid is an educational initiative dedicated to advancing knowledge and understanding of renewable energy systems and smart grid technologies.

### Project Overview

This project aims to:

- Provide accessible education about renewable energy
- Develop educational resources for students and professionals
- Foster collaboration between educational institutions and industry
- Promote sustainable energy practices
