## 🔆 Solar Teaching Kit

Complete lesson guide for teachers

This page provides all the resources you need to deliver an engaging solar energy lesson using our Solar Teaching Kit.

### What You'll Cover

Introduce students to how sunlight converts to electricity using photovoltaic cells. This hands-on experience helps students understand:

- How solar cells work
- The role of angles and shading
- Real-world solar applications
- Energy conversion and efficiency

### 📚 Materials Included

**Assembly Instructions**  
Clear step-by-step guide with a complete material list for building the solar cell experiment.

**Teacher Presentation**  
Ready-to-use slides that explain solar energy concepts and walk through the experiment.

**Student Worksheet**  
Guided experiments, observation questions, and calculation activities for students.

**Teacher Worksheet**  
Background information, teaching notes, expected results, and answer key.

### 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

- Explain how photovoltaic cells convert light into electricity
- Measure voltage and current in a solar system
- Analyze the impact of angle and shading on solar output
- Discuss real-world applications of solar technology
