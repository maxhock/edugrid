## Frequently Asked Questions

**How can I give feedback?**  
Please provide your feedback via the [contact form](/get-involved/contact/){: .btn .btn-outline}.

**What devices do students need?**  
A PC, cell phone, tablet or laptop with a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge) is sufficient. You can also print out the entire worksheet and give the lecture without access to the website.

**Where can I find the worksheet for students?**  
The worksheet (answer form) is available under "Downloads" on the teacher-platform. Teachers download it and distribute it in class.

**What additional supplies do I need?**  
Besides the components in the kit, you'll generally need basic tools like scissors, a screwdriver, and adhesive tape. The exact list is in the assembly guide.
