## Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find answers to common questions:

**Q: How can I access the educational materials?**  
A: All our educational resources are freely available on our platform. Simply browse the resources section.

**Q: Can my school or university become a partner?**  
A: Yes! We welcome educational institutions as partners. Please contact our coordination team to discuss opportunities.

**Q: Are the materials available in multiple languages?**  
A: We are working on translating materials into multiple European languages. Check our resources page for current language availability.

**Q: How can students get involved?**  
A: Students can participate through their educational institutions or contact us directly about student opportunities.
