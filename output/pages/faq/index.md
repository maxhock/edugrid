# Frequently Asked Questions

How can I give feedback?

Please provide your feedback via the contact form.

What devices do students need?

A PC, cell phone, tablet or laptop with a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge) is sufficient. You can also print out the entire worksheet and give the lecture without access to the website.

Where can I find the worksheet for students?

The worksheet (answer form) is available under “Downloads” on the teacher-platform. Teachers download it and distribute it in class.

What additional supplies do I need?

Besides the components in the kit, you’ll generally need basic tools like scissors, a screwdriver, and adhesive tape. The exact list is in the assembly guide.

What are the “kits” and why do I need them?

Kits include all the physical parts and materials students need for certain exercises (e.g., solar kit).

How do I assemble the kits?

Each kit includes a full materials list and the required 3D printable components on Thigiverse, as well as step-by-step instructions on [Instructables.com](http://instructables.com/). The links can be found in the teacher resource hub.

How can I support you?

We are always happy to have supporters. Simply contact us using the contact form and we will find out together how you can best help us.
