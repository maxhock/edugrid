## What data do we collect?

**EduGrid** collects the following data:

- Personal identification information via the contact form: Name, email address, phone number
- Automatically collected information: IP address and generic website analytics (via WordPress built-in analytics)

## How do we collect your data?

You directly provide **EduGrid** with most of the data via the **contact form**.

We also automatically collect data through **WordPress analytics** whenever you visit the website.

## How will we use your data?

**EduGrid** uses the collected data to:

- Respond to inquiries sent through the contact form
- Analyze generic website traffic to improve the website

No other uses, marketing, or sharing of your personal data takes place.

## How do we store your data?

Your data is securely stored on **Strato EU-based servers**. Security measures include SSL encryption, password-protected access, and the hosting provider’s security systems.

We keep contact form submissions and analytics data for **6 months**, after which it is deleted.

## Marketing

**EduGrid** does not use your data for marketing purposes, and data is not shared with any partners for marketing.

## What are your data protection rights?

You have the following rights under GDPR:

- **Access** – Request copies of your personal data.
- **Rectification** – Request correction or completion of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Erasure** – Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
- **Restriction of processing** – Request limitations on how your data is processed.
- **Objection** – Object to processing under certain conditions.
- **Data portability** – Request transfer of your data to another organization or to yourself.

Requests are handled within **one month**.

**Contact for data rights requests:**  
Email: [info@edu-grid.org](mailto:info@edu-grid.org)

## Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior.

**How we use cookies:**

- WordPress built-in analytics cookies to understand website usage.

**Types of cookies used:**

- Essential/Functionality – Necessary for website operation
- Analytics – Generic usage analysis via WordPress

**Managing cookies:**  
You can configure your browser to refuse cookies. Some website features may not function if cookies are disabled.
