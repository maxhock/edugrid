## ☀️ Solar Teaching Kit

Welcome to the Solar Teaching Kit! We are excited to support you in bringing hands-on solar energy education into your classroom.

> ⚠️ **Please complete Steps 1–3 a bit in advance** to ensure the Teaching Kit is ready before your lesson.

---

### 🔧 Step 1: Preparing your Kit

To begin, please collect all required materials to assemble your Solar Teaching Kit. You can view the complete Bill of Materials and the required 3D-printable components at the following links:

- 🔗 [View Bill of Materials on Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/)
- 🔗 [View Potibox on Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/)

---

### 🛠️ Step 2: Assembling the Kit

If you already have all components available, you may proceed with the assembly. Download the step-by-step instructions via the link below:

- 🔗 [Assembly Instructions on Instructables.com](https://www.instructables.com/)

---
