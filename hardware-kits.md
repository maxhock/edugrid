## Hardware Kits

*Open-source, 3D-printable kits that bring renewable energy into every classroom.*

Our kits are designed at TU Munich and released as open-source hardware. All 3D-printable files are available on Thingiverse and full assembly guides are published on Instructables — free for anyone to download and build.

## Why Open-Source Hardware?

We believe educational tools should be accessible to everyone. By publishing all designs under open-source licenses, any school, makerspace, or individual can:

- **Print** the components on any standard FDM 3D printer
- **Modify** the designs to fit local needs and materials
- **Share** improvements back with the community

> All kits are designed for students aged 14–18 and require no prior knowledge of electrical engineering to assemble or use.
