## Frequently Asked Questions

### How can I give feedback?

Please provide your feedback via the [contact form](/contact/).

### What devices do students need?

A PC, cell phone, tablet or laptop with a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge) is sufficient. You can also print out the entire worksheet and give the lecture without access to the website.

### Where can I find the worksheet for students?

The worksheet (answer form) is available under the student kits pages. Teachers can download it and distribute it in class.

### What additional supplies do I need?

Besides the components in the kit, you'll generally need basic tools like scissors, a screwdriver, and adhesive tape. The exact list is in the assembly guide included with each kit.

### What are the "kits" and why do I need them?

Kits include all the physical parts and materials students need for certain exercises (e.g., solar kit, wind kit, hydro kit). They provide a structured, hands-on learning experience that connects theory with practical experimentation.

### How do I assemble the kits?

Each kit includes:
- A full materials list
- 3D printable components available on [Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/)
- Step-by-step assembly instructions on [Instructables.com](https://www.instructables.com/)

The direct links can be found in the teacher resource hubs for each kit.

### Who is EduGrid for?

Our kits are designed for students aged 14-18 and work for both classroom settings and independent learning. Teachers, makerspaces, and educational clubs can all benefit from our materials.

### Are the materials open-source?

Yes! EduGrid embraces an open-source, DIY spirit. Our materials are designed to be reproduced and adapted by educators and learners worldwide.

### How can I support you?

We are always happy to have supporters. Simply [contact us](/contact/) using the contact form and we will find out together how you can best help us. Learn more about partnerships and sponsorship on our [Support Us](/support/) page.

### Do I need electrical engineering knowledge to teach these kits?

No! Our materials are designed to be accessible to all educators. While advanced students can dive deeper into concepts like efficiency calculations and data analysis, the core lessons are designed for anyone to teach.
