## 💧 Hydro Teaching Kit

We are excited to support you in bringing hands-on hydro energy education into your classroom.

**⚠️ Please complete Steps 1–3 in advance to ensure the kit is ready before your lesson.**

* * *

### 🔧 Step 1: Preparing Your Kit

Collect all materials needed to build the Hydro Energy Teaching Kit.

🔗 [View Bill of Materials on Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:7093144)

### 🛠️ Step 2: Assembling the Kit

If all components are available, proceed with the assembly using these instructions:

🔗 [Assembly Instructions on Instructables](#)

Gather basic classroom tools required for the experiments.

Each student should receive a printed copy of the water wheel model. They will build their own during the session as part of the workbook tasks.

- [📄 Water Wheel Model A4 (PDF)](https://edu-grid.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Water_Wheel_Model_A4.pdf)
- [📄 Water Wheel Model A3 (PDF)](https://edu-grid.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Water_Wheel_Model_A3.pdf)

### 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

- Understand how water flow generates electricity
- Build and test a water wheel turbine
- Analyze efficiency factors in hydropower systems
- Discuss real-world hydropower applications
